The Barber

Fed up with soulless bars and the disappearance of traditional barbers, we set out to recover the spirit of the barbershops of yore. And we resolved to open a space dedicated to rediscovering these essential bastions of masculinity.

The Whiskey

At our whiskey bar we believe in two things: Dedication and Respect, this is why we will be the best whiskey bar in the country.

The Style

Exclusive distributors of Uppercut. A brand established by barbers, that follows the timeless tenets of style; Uppercut represents the best in grooming products for men.

The Bartender

All of our bartenders are experienced and knowledgable with whiskey. Many of them, as well as our barbers, are dedicated professionals that have many years of experience in their respective trades.

The Chair

There are an abundant variety of barber’s chairs in the world but Belmont chairs are renown for their quality and tradition; they dominated the market during the golden-age of barbershops. Now, thanks to Takara and Hedbanger everyone can share this experience.

The Beer

Beer is an essential part of Hedbanger, which is why we have only partnered with those brands that share the Hedbanger spirit.
